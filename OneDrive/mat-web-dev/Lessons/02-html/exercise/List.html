creamy-tomato-courgetti

list with items need to buy

  • 4 slices of Parma ham
  • Â½ small pack basil
  • 350g tomato and mascarpone sauce
  • 250g pack courgetti

instructions to make that recipe

  1. Roughly tear the ham and basil.
  2. Heat a frying pan over a medium heat and dry-fry the ham until crisp.
  3. Transfer to a plate with a slotted spoon.
  4. Add the sauce to the pan and cook for 1-2 mins, then toss in the courgetti.
  5. Cook for 1 min more until warmed through.
  6. Divide between bowls, then top with the ham and basil.
