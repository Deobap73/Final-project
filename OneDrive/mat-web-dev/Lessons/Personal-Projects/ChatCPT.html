Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of chicken breast
  • 1 cup of uncooked white rice
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1/2 tsp of chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Add the chicken to the pan and season with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until browned on both sides and cooked through.
  4. Add the black beans and cooked rice to the pan and stir to combine.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
